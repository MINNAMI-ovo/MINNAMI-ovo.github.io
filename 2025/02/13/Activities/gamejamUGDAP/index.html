Activities:2025UGDAP GameDevelopmentPracticalProject

发表于2025-02-13|更新于2025-10-08|Activities
|浏览量:

Writing this blog post a few days later—I’ve been dabbling in game development since high school, off and on, and in my first semester of university, I signed up for a development training program!

This can pretty much be considered my first “game jam” experience.





Over the six days of training, I got my first complete exposure to every aspect of game development. I was honored to take on the role of programmer in our team. And within just three days (excluding lecture time), we used RPG Maker MV to create a 20-minute horror puzzle game——————【蜜月褪色公式】（Honeymoon Fading Formula.）And I have made a post so that you can install it and play



2025寒假游戏开发实训第二期作品精彩回顾


In short, it was a novel and truly joyful experience—I’ll definitely take part in more Game Jams in the future!:D

写下这篇博客的时候已经是几天后了，从高中就开始断断续续的接触游戏制作，在上大学的第一个学期就报名了开发实训！

也算是我接触的第一款“game jam”





在6天实训中，算是第一次完整接触了游戏制作的每个方面。我很荣幸的在小组中担任程序的职位。并且在除去讲课的3天内，用RPGMakerMV做出了流程20min的恐怖解密游戏——————【蜜月褪色公式】

以后还要做好多好多游戏！！！
