☆Musings

As a retired shut-in, the progress of many ideas has been slow. Half a year after ending my reclusive life, I finally managed to build my first blog. Whether it was modifying configuration files, adding settings, or using developer tools to troubleshoot issues, everything felt incredibly challenging for me. But if you’re reading this, it means all those difficulties have been overcome!

Perhaps in 2025, building a personal blog isn’t the most common or convenient way to share thoughts, but creating a little space of my own on the internet is still a delightful thing. It’s like having a corner of the noisy online world that belongs solely to me.

n a forum I created on Feiwen, the pinned post reads: “If everyone had a mental space, mine would be the sky on the verge of darkness.” I don’t like overly intense sunlight, so the sun forever lingering at the moment just before dawn is perfect.

This blog could be considered the only place in the sunlit internet realm where the light doesn’t reach.

hope you enjoy your time here.

☆碎碎念

作为一名退役家里蹲，很多想法实施的进度都慢慢的。在结束家里蹲的半年后成功搭建起来了自己的第一个博客。无论是更改配置文件和增加配置，使用开发者工具查找问题对我而言都非常的艰难，但能看到这篇意味着所有的困呐都迎刃而解了！

或许在2025年，自己搭建博客并不是一个常见且方便的传输思想的媒体，但是在网络上搭建一个属于自己的小空间也是非常惬意的事情。像是在嘈杂的互联网上拥有了 一处独属于自己的天地。

在废文我创建的论坛中，置顶的一段话是：“如果每个人都有心灵空间的话，我应该是将黑未黑的天边。”我不喜欢过于热烈的太阳，于是太阳就永远停留在升起但未破晓 的时刻最好。

这个博客也称得上日不落的互联网国度中唯一没有日光的地方吧。

祝你看的开心，逃离了人群，你只是需要一个地方躲起来。
